# APPENDIX H
# USER'S MANUAL


### 1. Registration and Onboarding

**Overview:** Create your account and complete the personalized onboarding process to set up your health profile. This ensures the meal planner provides recommendations tailored to your specific needs, dietary preferences, and health goals.

**How to Use:**
1. Open the SmartDiet app and tap **Register** on the welcome screen
2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
3. Complete the onboarding flow by providing:
   - **Basic Information:** Name, age, gender, height, and weight
   - **Health Goals:** Select from Weight Loss, Weight Gain, Maintain Weight, or Muscle Building
   - **Dietary Preferences:** Choose your diet type (Vegetarian, Vegan, Keto, etc.)
   - **Allergens:** Select any food allergies or sensitivities
   - **Activity Level:** Set your activity level from Sedentary to Very Active
   - **Meal Preferences:** Choose preferred meal times and types
4. Review your profile summary and confirm
5. Your profile will be saved to the system and used for personalized meal planning

---

### 2. Log In

**Overview:** Access the system using your registered credentials. This feature ensures secure access to your personalized meal plans, nutrition analytics, and saved recipes.

**How to Use:** 
1. Enter your email and password
2. Tap the **Login** button
3. You will be redirected to the Main Menu
4. If login fails, verify your credentials or use the **Forgot Password** option
5. For admin users, you will have access to additional administrative features

---

## User Features

### a. Recipe Search

**Overview:** Search and discover thousands of recipes from multiple sources including external APIs and the Filipino Recipe Database. View detailed nutrition information, ingredients, and cooking instructions.

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to **Recipe Search** from the main menu
2. Use the **search bar** to find recipes by name or ingredients
3. Apply **filters** to refine results:
   - Meal Type (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks)
   - Cuisine Type (Filipino, Asian, Mediterranean, etc.)
   - Dietary Restrictions (Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-Free, etc.)
   - Maximum Cooking Time
4. Browse search results with:
   - Recipe thumbnail images
   - Cooking time and serving information
   - Quick nutrition preview
   - Allergen warnings (if applicable)
5. Tap any recipe card to view **full details**:
   - Complete ingredient list with quantities
   - Step-by-step cooking instructions
   - Detailed nutrition breakdown (calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, sugar)
   - Allergen detection with ML-powered analysis
   - Ingredient substitution suggestions
6. From the recipe detail page, you can:
   - **Add to Favorites:** Save the recipe for quick access
   - **Add to Meal Planner:** Schedule it for a specific date and meal type
   - **Generate Shopping List:** Extract ingredients for shopping
   - **Share Recipe:** Share with friends or family

---

### b. Meal Planner Calendar

**Overview:** Manage your weekly meal schedule in an interactive calendar view. Add, edit, view, and delete meals for each day and meal type.

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to **Meal Planner** from the main menu
2. View your **weekly calendar** displaying all scheduled meals
3. **Navigate weeks** using the Previous/Next buttons
4. **Add a new meal:**
   - Tap a date and meal type (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
   - Search and select a recipe
   - Set the meal time
   - Tap **Save** to add to your calendar
5. **View meal details:** Tap any scheduled meal to see recipe information and nutrition
6. **Edit a meal:** 
   - Select the meal from the calendar
   - Modify the recipe or time
   - Save changes
7. **Delete a meal:**
   - Tap the meal to open details
   - Select **Delete**
   - Confirm deletion
8. Your meal schedule automatically updates nutrition tracking data

---

### c. Nutrition Analytics

**Overview:** Track and analyze your nutritional intake with comprehensive weekly progress reports and personalized profile analysis. View detailed breakdowns of calories, macronutrients, and micronutrients.

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to **Nutrition Analytics** from the main menu
2. Choose between two tabs:

   **Weekly Progress Tab:**
   - View **weekly averages** for:
     - Total calories
     - Protein, carbohydrates, fat
     - Fiber and sugar intake
   - See **daily nutrition breakdown** for each day of the week
   - Compare actual intake vs. target goals with visual progress bars
   - Navigate to **previous/next weeks** to track historical data
   - Identify patterns and trends in your eating habits

   **Your Profile Tab:**
   - **Profile Overview Card:**
     - Age, gender, height, weight
     - BMI (Body Mass Index) and weight category
   - **Personalized Macro Analysis:**
     - Daily calorie target based on your BMR and activity level
     - Protein target with recommendations based on age, gender, and goals
     - Carbohydrate target with health condition considerations
     - Fat target for hormone production and nutrient absorption
     - Fiber target for digestive health
   - **Health Insights:**
     - AI-generated recommendations based on your profile
     - Special considerations for allergies and health conditions
     - Tips for achieving your health goals

3. Use this data to adjust your meal planning and make informed dietary decisions

---

### d. Favorite Meals

**Overview:** Save and organize your favorite recipes for quick access. Rate recipes, add personal notes, and manage your collection of go-to meals.

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to **Favorite Meals** from the main menu
2. Browse your saved favorite recipes displayed as cards
3. Each recipe card shows:
   - Recipe image
   - Title and basic information
   - Star rating (if rated)
   - Cooking time, servings, and cuisine
   - Allergen warnings and substitution badges
   - Personal notes (if added)
4. **View a recipe:** Tap the recipe card to open full details
5. **Rate a recipe:**
   - Tap the star icons (1-5 stars)
   - Your rating is saved automatically
6. **Delete a recipe:**
   - Tap the **delete icon** on the recipe card
   - Confirm deletion
   - The recipe will be removed from favorites
7. **Pull to refresh** to reload your favorites list
8. Favorited recipes include all substitutions and modifications you made

---

### e. Shopping List Generator

**Overview:** Automatically generate shopping lists from your planned meals. The system extracts all ingredients, groups them by category, adjusts quantities based on servings, and calculates estimated costs.

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to **Shopping List Generator** from the main menu
2. **Set the planning period:**
   - Select **start date** and **end date**
   - Default range is current week (7 days)
3. **Adjust servings per recipe** using the +/- buttons
4. **Select recipes:**
   - Tap **Load Planned Meals** to auto-load recipes from your meal calendar
   - Or tap **Add More Recipes** to manually browse and select additional recipes
   - View selected recipes in the list
   - Remove recipes by tapping the **remove icon**
5. Tap **Generate Shopping List**
6. The system will:
   - Extract all ingredients from selected recipes
   - Combine duplicate ingredients
   - Group items by category (Grains, Meat, Vegetables, Dairy, etc.)
   - Calculate total quantities based on servings
   - Estimate costs per item
7. You'll be automatically redirected to the **Shopping List Manager**
8. Alternatively, tap **Go to Shopping List Manager** to view saved lists without generating a new one

---

### f. Shopping List Management

**Overview:** Manage your shopping lists with interactive features including checking off items, editing quantities and prices, adding new items, and sharing lists with others.

**How to Use:**
1. Access via **Shopping List Generator** or navigate to **Shopping List Manager** directly
2. View the **shopping summary** card displaying:
   - Total estimated cost
   - Total items
   - Items bought (checked off)
   - Remaining items
3. **Check off items as bought:**
   - Tap the checkbox next to any item
   - Checked items are marked as purchased
   - Total cost and remaining items update automatically
4. **Edit an item:**
   - Tap the **edit icon** on any item
   - Modify name, quantity, unit, or price
   - Tap **Save** to update
5. **Delete an item:**
   - Tap the **delete icon**
   - Confirm deletion
6. **Add a new item:**
   - Tap the **+ Add Item** button
   - Enter item name, quantity, unit, and price
   - Tap **Add** to include in the list
7. **Toggle all items:** Tap the **checklist icon** in the header to check/uncheck all items at once
8. **Save your list:**
   - Lists are **auto-saved** to Firestore as you make changes
   - Manually save by tapping the **save icon** in the header
9. **Load a saved list:**
   - Tap the **folder icon** in the header
   - Select from your saved shopping lists
   - Choose to load or delete saved lists
10. **Share your list:**
    - Tap the **share icon** in the header
    - View the formatted shopping list text grouped by category
    - Tap **Copy** to copy to clipboard and share via messaging apps

### g. Expert Meal Plans

**Overview:** Access professionally curated meal plans designed by nutrition experts for specific health goals and dietary needs. Apply complete weekly meal plans directly to your calendar.

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to **Expert Meal Plans** from the main menu
2. Choose between two tabs:

   **Browse Plans Tab:**
   - View all available expert-curated meal plans
   - Use the **search bar** to find plans by name
   - Apply **filters:**
     - **Health Goal:** Weight Loss, Weight Gain, Muscle Building, Diabetes Management, Heart Health, etc.
     - **Diet Type:** Vegetarian, Vegan, Keto, Paleo, Mediterranean, etc.
   - Each plan card shows:
     - Plan name and description
     - Health goal and target audience
     - Duration (number of days)
     - Daily calorie target
     - Total number of meals
     - Sample meals preview
     - Expert verified badge
   - **View plan details:** Tap **View Details** to see the complete meal schedule
   - **Apply a plan:**
     1. Tap **Apply Plan**
     2. Select the **week** you want to apply it to
     3. Review the **replacement warning** (existing meals in that week will be deleted)
     4. Confirm to apply
     5. The system will delete existing meals and add all expert plan meals to your calendar
     6. Tap **View** in the success message to see your updated meal planner

   **My Applied Plans Tab:**
   - View meal plans you've previously applied
   - See plan status (Active or Completed)
   - Check application dates and week ranges
   - **View applied plan details** by tapping **View**
   - **Remove a plan** by tapping **Remove** and confirming

3. Expert meal plans are optimized for specific health goals and automatically match your dietary preferences

### h. Account Settings

**Overview:** Manage your profile information, update dietary preferences, modify allergens, and customize app settings.

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to **Account Settings** from the main menu
2. **Edit Profile:**
   - Update name, age, gender
   - Modify height and weight
   - Change profile photo
3. **Update Health Information:**
   - Adjust health goals
   - Change activity level
   - Update target weight (if applicable)
4. **Manage Dietary Preferences:**
   - Select or remove dietary restrictions
   - Update preferred cuisines
   - Set meal time preferences
5. **Update Allergens:**
   - Add new food allergies or sensitivities
   - Remove allergens that no longer apply
   - The system will automatically avoid these in meal planning
6. **Notification Settings:**
   - Enable/disable meal reminders
   - Set reminder times for each meal
   - Manage push notification preferences
7. **Privacy Settings:**
   - Change password
   - Manage data sharing preferences
8. Tap **Save Changes** to update your profile
9. All changes will immediately affect meal planning and recipe recommendations
